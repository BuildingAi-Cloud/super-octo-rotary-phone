# What is BuildSync?

BuildSync is a modern, multilingual facility management platform designed for residential and commercial properties. It streamlines operations for property managers, staff, and residents by providing:

- **Role-based dashboards** for owners, managers, staff, tenants, and residents
- **Amenity booking** and management
- **Maintenance request tracking**
- **Audit logs** for transparency
- **Community bulletin board**
- **Accessible, responsive UI** with dark mode and language selector
- **Multi-tenancy** and SaaS/on-premise support
- **Plugin system** for extensibility
- **Secure authentication** with MFA support
- **API documentation** for integrations

BuildSync helps property teams deliver better service, improve communication, and automate routine tasks—all in a secure, scalable, and user-friendly environment.
