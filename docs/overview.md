# Overview

Buildings.com is an AI-native and legacy building management platform for commercial and residential properties. It provides secure, scalable, and privacy-focused solutions for property owners, facility managers, building managers, support staff, and residents.

- SaaS and On-Premise deployment
- AI-driven automation and analytics
- Legacy system integration
- Privacy and security by design

# User Hierarchy

The platform is designed around a four-tier operational hierarchy. Each tier has a distinct SaaS persona, dashboard, and data-visibility scope.

## 1. Property Owner — "The Investor"

- **Role:** Strategic oversight. The building is a business unit — the Owner maximises yield while protecting the physical investment.
- **Dashboard Focus:** Portfolio health, ROI, NOI tracking, CapEx forecasting, occupancy & retention, risk/compliance, and the "Pulse" indicator (Green / Yellow / Red building health).
- **Authority:** Approves major capital expenditures (CapEx) and sets the annual budget.
- **SaaS Interaction:** High-level analytics, financial reports, and Pulse alerts for critical risks. Primarily a **view-only** consumer of data generated by managers below.
- **Key question:** *"Is my money safe and growing?"*

## 2. Facility Manager — "The Technical Lead"

- **Role:** Infrastructure and systems. Responsible for the physical health of the asset.
- **Dashboard Focus:** HVAC, electrical, plumbing, fire/life-safety, structural inspections, IoT sensor data, and vendor management.
- **Authority:** Manages technical vendors and maintenance crews. Reports to the Owner on asset condition and capital needs.
- **SaaS Interaction:** Scheduling preventative maintenance, monitoring sensor alerts, managing the asset lifecycle, and tracking hard-FM / soft-FM compliance.
- **Key question:** *"Is the equipment running and compliant?"*

## 3. Building Manager — "The Operator"

- **Role:** Daily operations and people. The bridge between the physical building and its occupants.
- **Dashboard Focus:** Tenant relations, move-ins/outs, communications, staff scheduling, amenity bookings, visitor management, packages, work orders, and vendor compliance.
- **Authority:** Directs front-of-house staff (concierge, security, cleaners). Acts as the platform **super-user** who manages day-to-day data.
- **SaaS Interaction:** Handling communication blasts, resolving resident tickets, managing visitor logs, onboarding/offboarding residents, and amenity operations.
- **Key question:** *"Are the tenants happy and is the building orderly?"*

## 4. Support Staff & Vendors — "The Execution Layer"

- **Role:** Specialised task execution.
- **Includes:** Concierge, security officers, cleaning staff, maintenance technicians, and external vendors.
- **Dashboard Focus:** Individual work orders, task lists, and mobile-first job-site views.
- **SaaS Interaction:** Check-in at a job site, upload a photo of a completed repair, close out a ticket, log visitor arrivals, and register deliveries.
- **Key question:** *"What do I need to do right now?"*

## Data Flow — How the Hierarchy Syncs

| Event | Execution Level | Reporting Level | Owner Visibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| A pipe bursts | Vendor closes the repair ticket | FM reviews cost & updates asset history | Owner sees a "Maintenance Expense" spike on the monthly budget |
| A tenant moves out | Concierge logs key return & elevator booking | BM triggers unit cleaning & security deposit refund | Owner sees "Vacancy" increase by 1% on the occupancy chart |
| Energy bill is high | IoT sensor flags high usage in common areas | FM investigates HVAC efficiency | Owner sees "Utility Costs" 10% over budget |
